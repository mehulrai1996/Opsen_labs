What it is

A full reset —
without starting over

Most products that reach scale were never engineered to handle it. What worked at 100 users becomes a liability at 10,000. The codebase that shipped fast is now the thing slowing everything down.

MVP Recovery is a structured engagement designed to identify exactly what's broken, what's holding you back, and what needs to be rebuilt — versus what can be safely improved in place.

We don't rip and replace for sport. We operate with surgical precision: maximum impact, minimum disruption to your live product and your users.

Warning signs

You need recovery
if any of this sounds familiar

Your engineers spend more time fixing bugs than shipping features. Deployments are risky events, not routine operations. New team members take weeks to become productive. Users are churning over performance issues your team has known about for months.

These are not engineering problems. They are business problems — and they compound until addressed. MVP Recovery stops the bleeding and lays the foundation for everything you want to build next.
